Frequently Asked Questions

What image formats are supported?

We support JPEG, PNG, and WebP formats. You can choose to keep the original format or convert to a different format during compression.

Is there a file size limit?

There's no strict limit, but very large images (over 10MB) may take longer to process depending on your device's capabilities.

How does the compression work?

Our tool uses modern browser APIs to optimize your images by adjusting quality parameters and using efficient encoding methods for each format.
